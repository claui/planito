Current Weather Layer Does Not Show

By default Planito accesses certain data sources with one username for all application users. Because of this, usage limits may be hit and some layers fail to load.

To completely solve this problem for Current Weather layer, perform the following steps:

  1. Go to http://geonames.org and register free account
  2. On your account page enable your account to access the web service
  3. Open Terminal and execute defaults write com.mifki.Planito GeoNamesUser your_geonames_username

It's recommended to do this even if you do not currently experience problems with Current Weather layer.
