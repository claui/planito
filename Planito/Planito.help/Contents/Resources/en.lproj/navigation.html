Navigating The Map

In Preferences you can enable or disable multitouch-style navigation. This setting affects trackpads only, not mice.

With multitouch-style navigation enabled: Use standard gestures to navigate the map (two-finger scrolling and pinch). Zooming also can be done by two-finger vertical scrolling with Alt key pressed.

With legacy-style navigation enabled: Left-click and drag the map to move it, and use two-finger vertical scrolling to zoom in and out.

With mouse: Left-click and drag the map to move it, and use scroll wheel to zoom in and out.
